Recipe of pizza

Why pizza?

pizza is popular for its delicious taste, versatility, convenience, ability to be shared, and its connection to culinary traditions. It's an indulgent and satisfying choice that can be enjoyed on different occasions with loved ones.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough: You can buy pre-made dough or make your own using flour, yeast, water, salt, and oil.
  • Tomato sauce: Use pizza sauce or make your own using crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices of your choice.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella is the classic cheese for pizza, but you can also use other types like cheddar, goat cheese, or blue cheese.
  • Additional toppings: You can add a variety of toppings based on your preferences. Some popular options include:
    • Pepperoni or salami
    • Mushrooms
    • Onion
    • Bell peppers
    • Olives
    • Ham
    • Pineapple
    • Fresh tomatoes
    • Spinach
    • Meatballs
    • Anchovies
    • Corn
    • Arugula
  • Herbs and spices: Add fresh herbs like basil, oregano, or thyme, as well as spices like garlic powder or paprika to enhance the flavor of the pizza.

Steps

  1. Prepare or buy pre-made pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a round or rectangular shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle grated cheese, preferably mozzarella, over the tomato sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, or others.
  6. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at a high temperature, around 200-230°C (400-450°F), for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  8. Optionally, you can add fresh herbs like basil or oregano, as well as a drizzle of olive oil over the pizza before serving.
