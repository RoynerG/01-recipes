Recipe of hamburger

Why hamburguer?

Hamburger is a food very special for my because have meat,cheeze,chicken and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheeze
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: hamburger meat, bread, vegetables, cheese and condiments.
  2. Cook the meat on a hot grill or skillet for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  3. Slice and toast the bread.
  4. Assemble the burger by placing the meat on the bun and adding the vegetables, cheese and condiments.
  5. Enjoy your burger! Serve with fries or other side dishes if desired.
